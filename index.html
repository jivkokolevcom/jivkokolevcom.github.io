About Me

I started off my career as applications developer back in the 90's. I worked at several startups - first in Bulgaria and then in US. In 2001 I started at Microsoft. I switched several divisions through the years - MS Office, AdCenter, and Developer's Division. In 2019 I worked for an year in robotic process automation company UiPath where I architected the cloud platform for the company's SaaS offering. Today I manage the organization at Microsoft that builds and supports the core platform services behind Azure DevOps - authentication, authorization, licensing, and commerce.
On the side, I stay current with industry trends by experimenting with them in my home lab. This is where I also run my home automation projects.
